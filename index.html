01

Intake & Validation

Physical inspection. Stock BIOS confirmed in GPU-Z. Full VRAM integrity sweep to catch bad memory before a single tool touches the card. FurMark thermal baseline. Photographed, serialized, and logged — front and back, every spec on file.

  • VisualFront & back macro
  • VRAMError-scan full-pass
  • ThermalBaseline captured
02

Thermal Re-Engineering

Full teardown. Factory paste stripped with 99% IPA and lint-free wipes. Thermal Grizzly Kryonaut applied to the die with precision. Every VRAM and VRM pad measured with calipers, replaced with Fujipoly XR-m. Reassembled with calibrated torque. Result: a permanent 10–20°C drop under sustained load.

  • PasteKryonaut
  • PadsFujipoly XR-m
  • TorqueCalibrated
03

Precision Performance Tuning

We open the voltage-frequency curve and find each chip's silicon-specific efficiency point through iterative stability testing. Power limits are set to the workload: 80% TDP for 24/7 AI inference, 90–95% for gaming burst. Custom fan curves keep the unit silent under light load. A 24-hour combined stress test confirms stability before we sign off.

  • UndervoltPer-silicon
  • TDPWorkload-tuned
  • Soak24h combined
